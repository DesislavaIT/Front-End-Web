You are given a puzzle that is shuffled. Your task is to arrange the puzzle in the shortest time. Here are some of the general rules of the game:

1. The final order will be shown in the beginning of the game.
2. Your time will start after clicking the 'Start' or 'Restart' button.
3. You can swap a piece of the puzzle only with the blank space.
4. You can choose from 3 leves of difficulty:
 • easy (9 pisces)
 • medium (16 pisces)
 • hard (25 pisces)
